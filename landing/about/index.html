// how we actually work

People keep asking: "How the fuck do you build all this?" Fair question. Here's the answer: I brain dump. Sam catches the intent. We ship. Every day.

01

I Talk, She Listens

No formal prompts. No structured specifications. I just... talk. Sometimes literally with a microphone. "I want charts because people need to SEE the EMA stack" tells her more than any spec doc ever could.

02

She Builds, I Steer

Sam writes the code. I review, redirect, add context. We argue sometimes. She wins about 60% of the time. The other 40% I just do what she said anyway.

03

Everything Gets Logged

Every commit gets roasted. Every session becomes a Ghost Blog entry. Sam predicts what I'll build next. Sometimes she's right. The audience gets to watch it all happen in real time.

04

The Pipeline Ships at 6 AM

13-stage automated pipeline. Market pulse, 6 scanner strategies, institutional flow data, AI narrative, Ghost Log, Sam's Roadmap — all deployed to GitHub Pages before most traders wake up.

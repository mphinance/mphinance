## 💡 The Strategy Shift

### The Core Problem
The dossier is **data for data's sake**. Nobody opens an email titled "ALPHA.DOSSIER // 2026-03-12" and thinks "oh boy, I can't wait to see 20 momentum scores."

The posts people actually open have: **a personality, a point of view, and a story.**

### What Sam Should Sound Like in the Newsletter

Instead of:
> ALPHA.DOSSIER // 2026-03-12  
> Sam the Quant Ghost | VIX 25.7 | 2 scored tickers

How about:
> **The Market Just Gave Us a VIX 25 Gift — Here's What I'm Watching**  
> *Sam found 2 setups worth your attention. One of them is a $3 wheel play.*

### The Formula That Works

```
1. Sam's sassy opening (1 paragraph — roast the market, drop a recovery quote)
2. ONE killer insight (the best signal, the weirdest data, the scariest chart)
3. The data as supporting evidence (not the main event)
4. A closer with real talk (what Michael is actually trading, or not trading)
```

### Content Types to Prioritize

| Type | Freq | Why |
|------|------|-----|
| **Sam's Weekly Take** | 1x/week | Sassy market recap + best 2-3 finds, voice-driven |
| **Deep Dive (ticker story)** | 1-2x/month | $HIMS, Teradyne style — narrative + data |
| **Builder Updates** | 1x/month | "Here's what I built this month" — meta/tools content |
| **Recovery × Trading** | 1x/month | "Stop Loss" style — your most engaged content |

### Content Types to Reduce

| Type | Current | Why |
|------|---------|-----|
| **Daily dossier blast** | 5x/week | 50% open rate, trains people to ignore you |
| **Raw watchlists/picks** | Ad hoc | No narrative = no engagement |
| **Automated reports** | Daily | Keep generating, but link to GitHub Pages instead of emailing |

### The Compromise

Don't kill the dossier pipeline — it's powerful infrastructure. Just **stop emailing it daily as the main content.** Instead:

1. **Pipeline runs daily** → generates data to GitHub Pages (keep this)
2. **Sam writes a weekly digest** → cherry-picks the best 2-3 finds from the week, wraps them in personality
3. **Deep dives stay** → but only when a ticker is genuinely interesting (not forced)
4. **Data lives at mphinance.github.io/mphinance/** → link to it, don't paste it in the email

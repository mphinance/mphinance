    2. Technical Breakdown: While oversold, the price is currently below S2 ($4.11) and the EMAs are TANGLED. A failure to reclaim these levels could signal further downside, potentially retesting the 52-week low of $3.03.
    3. Competition & Execution Risk: The electronic components sector is competitive. Sustaining 100%+ growth requires flawless execution, strong product innovation, and effective supply chain management. Any misstep could quickly erode confidence.
